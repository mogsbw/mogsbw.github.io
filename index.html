Welcome to Prescriptle! Here's how to play:

Guess the prescription using the onscreen keyboard. This is an example of a valid guess: +5.25/–1.75x005.

The first character is either + or –. Keep in mind there can be multiple of the same number and that axis must be less than (or equal to) 180!

For the wider boxes make sure to use the designated "00", "25", "50" or "75" buttons.

A green box means that the character in place. Yellow means the right character but in the wrong spot. Grey means it's wrong.

Try to guess the right prescription in 6 guesses. Good luck!
