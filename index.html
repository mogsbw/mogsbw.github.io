Welcome to 2wo Fat Girls! Fat Girls with Skinny Wallets – so in true millennial style, what better way is there to spend money than on that smashed avo?! Join us on our adventure as you watch our wallets thin, food babies grow – all documented in this blog, full of humble, yet honest ramblings.
